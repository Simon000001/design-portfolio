Our Story

A small room, a big obsession

ROAST began with a single drum roaster in a borrowed back room and a stubborn belief that a great cup of coffee can quietly change your whole day.

Every bean is ethically sourced in small batches from Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, then roasted here on the bench. Every pastry is laminated, folded, and baked in-house by hand.

Whether you're here for a morning pour over, an afternoon pastry, or a quiet evening corner to read — pull up a chair and stay awhile.

— Elena & Marcus, founders
