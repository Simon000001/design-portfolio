Our Story

Haven & Brew was born from a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee can change your day. Founded in the heart of the city by two friends who traded corporate desks for espresso machines, we’ve spent years perfecting our craft.

Every bean we roast is ethically sourced from small-batch farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Every pastry is laminated, folded, and baked in-house by hand. We believe in slow food, slow coffee, and genuine connection.

Whether you’re here for a morning pour-over, an afternoon pastry, or an evening corner to read, you’re family. Pull up a chair and stay awhile.

— Elena & Marcus, Founders
