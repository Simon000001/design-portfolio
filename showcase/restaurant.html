The Kitchen

A Single Table, A Single Vision

The Pass · 18 seats nightly

Cooking as Conversation

GEM serves a single nightly tasting to eighteen guests. There is no à la carte, no second seating — only one room, one menu, and one rhythm that unfolds over the course of an evening.

Our kitchen is led by a chef who built this room around a simple idea: that a meal should feel like a conversation between the land, the season, and the people seated at the counter. Ingredients arrive each morning; the menu is written each afternoon.

"We don't chase perfection. We chase the moment a dish feels honest — then we send it out."

The Evening

How GEM Works

One Seating

A single nightly service. Doors at seven; the evening unfolds at its own unhurried pace.

Pairings

An optional flight from our cellar, chosen by the sommelier to follow each course.

Seasonal
