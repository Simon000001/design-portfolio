Design experiments

Each experiment explores a specific design principle through interactive, real-time applications.

001 · Interaction

Responsive Cursor

An exploration of dynamic cursor feedback that responds to underlying content density, color, and motion — creating a tactile sense of digital presence.

002 · Layout

Modular Grid System

A generative layout engine based on Swiss grid principles, dynamically rearranging content blocks while maintaining rhythmic visual hierarchy.

003 · Motion

Kinetic Type

Variable-weight typography that responds to scroll velocity and ambient cursor motion, bridging the gap between static type and living form.

004 · Color

Chromatic Shift

Real-time color palette generation using perceptual color spaces, creating harmonious gradients that adapt to user interaction patterns.

005 · Data

Ambient Data

Transforming live data streams into ambient visual textures — information design as atmospheric backdrop rather than explicit dashboard.

006 · Sound

Visual Resonance

Audio-reactive visual systems that translate frequency, amplitude, and harmonic structures into geometric compositions in real time.

Process

Our approach

We operate at the intersection of Swiss design tradition and emerging technology. Our experimental space is dedicated to exploring interaction models, typographic systems, and generative aesthetics — with rigorous attention to craft, structure, and clarity.

Every experiment begins with a question about how people perceive and interact with digital interfaces. Through iterative prototyping, user observation, and cross-disciplinary collaboration, we develop novel interaction patterns that expand the vocabulary of screen-based design.
